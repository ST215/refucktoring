# Sierpinski Triangle

Test with ```pytest sierpinski_test.py```

## Task
Produce an ASCII representation of a Sierpinski triangle (a fractal) of order N.

## Example
The Sierpinski triangle of order 4 should look like this:
```

                       *
                      * *
                     *   *
                    * * * *
                   *       *
                  * *     * *
                 *   *   *   *
                * * * * * * * *
               *               *
              * *             * *
             *   *           *   *
            * * * *         * * * *
           *       *       *       *
          * *     * *     * *     * *
         *   *   *   *   *   *   *   *
        * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *
```
